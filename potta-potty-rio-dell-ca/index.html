Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need porta-potties for a small event at home?

Yes, and you’ll be glad you had them. Small gatherings can overwhelm your home’s plumbing. Having a clean, separate facility for guests saves your restroom from overuse and keeps everyone more comfortable.

Do you service the units during the rental period?

Yes, we service our units according to your schedule. This includes cleaning, restocking, and general upkeep to make sure everything stays hygienic and ready to use for as long as you have them.

Can I book a porta potty just a day ahead?

Yes, you can. If you suddenly realize you need a porta potty for tomorrow’s event or project, just reach out to us. As long as we have units available, we’ll deliver on time.

Can I request more porta-potties if my guest count increases unexpectedly?

Of course. We understand that guest numbers can sometimes go beyond what was planned. If you ever find yourself in that situation, just give us a call. We’ll arrange for additional units to be delivered quickly.

Do your porta-potties start to smell after multiple uses?

Not ours. All our units are properly ventilated and treated with eco-friendly deodorizing solutions that control odor, even with heavy use. Besides, we schedule service and restock the units during the rental period.

Can I rent your hand sanitizers or portable sinks without renting restrooms?

Yes, you can. Our portable sinks and hand sanitizer stations are available as standalone rentals.

Rio Dell, CA Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Rio Dell (Spanish: Río Dell, meaning "Dell River") is a city in Humboldt County, California, United States. It is located on the west bank of the Eel River 1 mile (2 km) north of Scotia at an elevation of 161 feet (49 m). The population was 3,363 at the 2010 census, up from 3,174 at the 2000 census.
